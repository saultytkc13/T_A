About TenderAI

Helping India's SMEs Win More Tenders

Every day, crores worth of government contracts go to companies who simply knew what to bid on. TenderAI gives every small business that same edge — instantly, affordably, and accurately.

⚠️ The Problem

Tender review is slow, complex & expensive

Thousands of tenders are published daily across government portals. Most SMEs either miss them entirely or waste hours manually reading PDFs — only to find they weren't eligible to begin with.

✅ The Solution

AI that reads the tender so you don't have to

Upload the PDF. In seconds, TenderAI tells you your eligibility score, red flags, required documents, and whether you should bid — backed by direct citations from the tender itself.

Our Mission

India has millions of capable SMEs, contractors, and builders who deserve a fair shot at government contracts. But the tender process has always been tilted toward large firms with dedicated legal and procurement teams.

TenderAI levels the playing field. By automating the most time-consuming part of tendering — reading and evaluating documents — we give small businesses the same decision-making power as companies 10x their size. Compete smarter, not harder.
